# AutoStream Product Information

## Pricing & Features
### Basic Plan
- **Cost**: $29/month
- **Limit**: 10 videos/month
- **Resolution**: 720p
- **Support**: Standard email support

### Pro Plan
- **Cost**: $79/month
- **Limit**: Unlimited videos
- **Resolution**: 4K resolution
- **Features**: AI captions included
- **Support**: 24/7 priority support

## Company Policies
- **Refunds**: No refunds after 7 days of purchase.
- **Support Availability**: 24/7 live support is available ONLY for Pro plan subscribers. Basic plan users get response within 24 hours.
